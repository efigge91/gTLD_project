Summary

This project analyzed the effect that latitude has on weather. We assembled a dataset of 500 cities from the OpenWeatherMap API. We then compared the latitude of these cities with their maximum temperature, average humidity, average cloudiness, and wind speed.

We used Matplotlib to visualize the data after pulling the data from the API. This site provides those visulizations as well as the source data for the project. In addition, there is explanations and descriptions of the trends displayed by the visualizaitons.

  • Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
  • Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
  • Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
  • Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude
